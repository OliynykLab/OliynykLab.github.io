Our high school students Joseph Oziel and Yujing Sun gave poster and oral presentations regarding their work over the last school year as well at the [2025 POSHSS](https://pioneersofstem.org/program/)! Yujing presented on "Exploring Feature Engineering for Crystal Structure Classification: Interactive Applications of PCA and PLS-DA Clustering" and Joseph presented on both "Improving Structural Predictions with KNN-Enhanced SISSO Models" and "Systematic Ternary Exploration of Gd–Os–Ge with Novel Phases"
